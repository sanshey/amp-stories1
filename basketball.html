He collapsed on the court and never played again

By Jugaad Journalist

Maurice Stokes was named NBA Rookie of the Year in 1955-56

And then went on to be a part of the NBA All Star team three times

He collided with the basketball poll in the last game of the 1958 season

The accident paralysed him & he went unconscious

He never played basketball again

The Jersey No.12 was retired by his team Sacramento Kings

In 1958, a charity exhibition game was organised to help raise funds for Stokes to meet his medical expenses

It became an annual thing and was named Maurice Stokes Memorial Basketball Game

He died of a heart attack at the age of 36 having played just three seasons of NBA in 1970

After his death, St. Francis University Campus opened The Maurice Stokes Athletics Centre to honour him in 1971
